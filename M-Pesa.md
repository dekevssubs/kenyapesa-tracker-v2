## üõçÔ∏è 3) **Buy Goods (Business Till)**

* **Customers are charged 0 fees** for most Buy Goods payments (payments to a Till number).

## üßæ 4) **PayBill Payments**

## üè¶ 5) **Bank Transfers**

* **M-Pesa ‚Üí Bank Account**: There may be a small fee (varies by bank), but in recent M-Pesa tariff structures sending to banks is often treated like a transfer with minimal to moderate fees (similar to P2P bands or a bank-specific fee).
* **Bank ‚Üí M-Pesa**: Often free or subject to bank fees (not Safaricom). *(Note: exact bank charges vary.)*

üìå You should confirm the specific bank fees in the ‚ÄúMy Safaricom App‚Äù or bank tariff guide.

## üìà 6) **M-Shwari (Loans & Savings)**

M-Shwari is a banking product *within* M-Pesa and has separate fees:

### üîπ **Loan Charges**

* A **one-off facility fee of ~7.5% of the loan amount** plus applicable excise duty (typically ~1.5%) is charged when a loan is disbursed. ([Safaricom][5])

### üîπ **Savings**

* **No charge** to deposit or move money between M-Pesa and M-Shwari. Savings can earn interest, and there are usually no transaction fees for savings movement. ([NCBA Group][6])

*(Note: Specific charges, interest, and terms are set by NCBA Bank under M-Shwari terms.)*

---

## ‚è±Ô∏è 7) **Fuliza (Overdraft)**

Fuliza lets you complete transactions even with insufficient balance, but **fees apply**:

### üìç **Fuliza Charges**

* **Access fee:** ~**1%** of the amount you use (one-off).
* **Daily maintenance fee:** Charged on outstanding balance daily. Amount varies by the overdraft amount (small to large bands).
* Some promotional/waiver structures exist (e.g., first few days free on small sums). ([Safaricom][7])

**Example Structure (indicative):**

* 101‚Äì500 KES outstanding ‚Üí ~2.5 KES + excise duty daily
* 501‚Äì1,000 KES ‚Üí ~5 KES + excise daily
  *(Actual fees vary by outstanding band and repayment timing.)* ([Safaricom][7])

üìå Fuliza is repaid automatically once you top up your M-Pesa balance.

## üÜì **Free or Zero Fees (Common)**

* Changing M-Pesa PIN
* Sending KSh **1‚Äì100** to another registered user
* Buy Goods (Till) for customers (in most cases) ([Kenyantimes][1])
